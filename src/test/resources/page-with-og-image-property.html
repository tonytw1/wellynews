Holidays start this weekend…Holiday Programme next week.

By wjjaApril 2, 2023News
No Comments
Location: City Dojo, 132 Hutt Road, Kaiwharawhara. Access off Westminster Street.

Dates: Tuesday 11 – Friday 14 April. 4 days.

Times: 9.00am – 3.00pm

Eligible: Kids 6-13 years. Experienced judoka, raw beginners and everything in between.

Students need to bring: Judogi and Belt, Food for the day, water bottle, rash top or extra tee shirt, medication as needed.
We can loan dogi and belt for those who are new to judo.

Cost:

5 Days – $120
4 Days – $100
3 Days – $80
2 Days – $60
1 Days – $40

All subsequent family members get $20 discount.

At least 15 kids are needed to make this fly.

Please email info@wellingtonjudo.org.nz for bookings or questions.
